Our Mission

Last summer, 6 high school students wanted to know how asthma, stress, and environment impacted residents in Richmond. The prevalence of asthma in CA is around 13% and in Richmond, it’s almost 25%! The students found that neighborhoods with fewer disorders (e.g., litter, alcohol bottles, cigarettes) had higher incomes and fewer asthma emergency visits, whereas neighborhoods with more disorders had lower incomes and increased asthma Emergency visits.

With this mural, we want others to see that Richmond has a lot of assets and that disorders can be decreased.
Our hope with this mural is that it motivates local youths and community members to create and maintain a better Richmond for everyone to enjoy.
